How the Hub Works

Three Ways to
Plug In.

The LINK Community Hub operates across three engagement levels. Choose the track that fits your organization, and we'll connect you to the right people, programs, and platforms.

01

Community Partner (Nonprofit / Civic)

For nonprofits, community organizations, and civic groups. Access roundtables, resource-sharing events, collaborative programming, and visibility in the LINK ecosystem. Contact us to discuss your organization's fit.

02

Vetted Business Partner

For small businesses and service providers. Apply to be listed in the LINK Vetted Directory — annually curated, community-backed, and visible to the entire LINK membership and network. Plans from $250/yr.

03

Viewer Membership

For individuals, executives, and professionals who want access to LINK resources, events, the Vetted Directory, and the broader LINK community network. Monthly and annual plans available from $47/mo.
