Affiliate Model

How the LINK Referral Revenue Model Works

LINK Lake Norman™ is built on relationships and trust. When we refer business to you, we earn a small referral fee — and you set the terms. Choose a project-based rate or an annual percentage of revenue wins.

📌 Option A — Per-Project Referral Fee
You pay LINK a percentage of the contract value each time a referred client signs with you. Set your own rate in your member portal.
Referral AmountExample RateLINK Earns
$500 project10%$50
$2,500 project8%$200
$10,000 project5%$500
$25,000+ projectCustomNegotiated
You set your own rates. LINK invoices quarterly for referral fees earned.
📈 Option B — Annual Revenue Share
Preferred by long-term partners. Pay LINK a flat annual percentage of total revenue generated through LINK referrals over the membership year.
Partner Revenue TierLINK % RateEst. Annual
Under $50K in wins3%Up to $1,500
$50K–$150K in wins4%$2K–$6K
$150K+ in wins5%Custom
Executive PartnersCustomNegotiated
Reported via member portal. Annual reconciliation with LINK before renewal.
📊 How Referrals Are Tracked
Everything is transparent, simple, and stored in your Google Sheet dashboard.
  • ✅ LINK sends you a referral via email with a unique referral code
  • ✅ You log the outcome in your member portal (won / pending / lost)
  • ✅ Revenue data auto-syncs to LINK's Google Sheet
  • ✅ Quarterly summary emailed to both parties
  • ✅ You invoice or we invoice — your choice of structure
  • ✅ All history stored in your member dashboard
⚙️ You Control Your Terms
From your member portal, you can set and update your affiliate model at any time.
  • 🎯 Choose Option A (per-project) or Option B (annual %)
  • 💰 Set your own referral fee percentage
  • 📋 Define which services qualify for referral fees
  • 🚫 Exclude certain client types if needed
  • 📅 Set a cap on annual referral fee obligations
  • ✏️ Update terms at any annual renewal
